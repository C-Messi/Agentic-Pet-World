# Town Activities

- `fortune-draw`: invite up to three visible residents to draw and interpret a
  bounded, entertainment-only fortune.
- `social-play`: spend time playing with visible residents.
- `open-stall`: display one to three explicitly public personality items. Stalls
  have no prices, inventory, purchases, currency, or profit.
- `build`: use only the registered recipe and plot IDs supplied in the current
  authoritative town context.

Return stories must be first-person and grounded in persisted outing events.
